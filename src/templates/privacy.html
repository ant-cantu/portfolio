Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2025

Thank you for visiting my portfolio website. Your privacy is important to me. This policy explains how I collect, use, and protect any personal information you provide through the contact form on this website.

1. Information Collected

If you choose to contact me using the form, I may collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content

2. How Your Information Is Used

I use this information solely to respond to your inquiry or message. I do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Transmission

Please note that your message is transmitted electronically to me via a private Discord webhook. While I take reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Data Retention

I will retain your contact message for as long as needed to address your inquiry. After that, it will be deleted. If you wish to have your information removed, please contact me at contact@antcantu.com and I will promptly delete it.

5. Cookies and Analytics

This website does not use cookies to track personal information and does not run third-party advertising or analytics.

5a. Use of Cloudflare

This website uses Cloudflare, a content delivery network and security provider, to help protect and accelerate the website. Cloudflare may process visitors’ IP addresses and other data required to deliver its services. For more details, please see Cloudflare’s privacy policy at https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/.

5b. Rate Limiting

To protect this website from abusive or excessive requests, I use rate-limiting technology. This temporarily processes visitors’ IP addresses to limit repeated requests. No personal data beyond IP addresses is stored long-term as part of this process.

6. Your Rights

If you are located in the European Union or California, you may have additional rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Please email me at contact@antcantu.com to exercise those rights.

7. Changes

I may update this policy from time to time. Please check this page for any changes.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please email me at contact@antcantu.com.
