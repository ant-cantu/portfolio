About Me

Hi there! My name is Anthony. My tech stack includes C++, Python, JavaScript, C#, HTML, and CSS. I've applied these languages across a variety of projects featured on my portfolio, including applications that leverage modern APIs such as the OpenAI API and Google Gemini API. Through these projects, I've developed skills in prompt engineering, secure data handling, database management, and building scalable systems. I've also designed and implemented RESTful APIs to support structured, maintainable, and efficient backend services.

My ongoing projects, including a fantasy football web application that leverages AI-powered start/sit recommendations, reflect my dedication to applying hypothesis-driven development, design thinking, and agile principles in real-world scenarios. I thrive on problem-solving and feel a deep sense of accomplishment when delivering effective solutions to challenging problems.

As a committed lifelong learner, I actively explore new technologies through a combination of online resources and AI tools, structuring lesson plans and receiving iterative feedback to accelerate my growth. I am currently pursuing an A.S. in Computer Science while also continuously expanding my skills through self-directed projects and online learning. I am now seeking opportunities as a junior software developer or apprentice, where I can contribute to meaningful projects and collaborate with innovative teams. For a closer look at my work, I invite you to explore my GitHub, which showcases several of the projects I have developed.

My Projects

Fumbld AI

Fumbld AI is a fantasy football application that leverages the power of the Gemini to provide users with intelligent start/sit recommendations for their fantasy rosters. By connecting to popular fantasy platforms like Yahoo Sports and Sleeper, it fetches user-specific roster data and provides advice to help you dominate your league.

Source is available upon request

PetGPT

PetGPT is a Unity-based desktop pet that interacts with you using OpenAI's ChatGPT API. The project overlays a small animated companion on your desktop that you can drag around and chat with in real time.

My DMS

In June 2024, a cyberattack on CDK disrupted parts tracking and billing at my workplace. To keep operations running, I developed an application that logged sales data (part numbers, quantities, invoice numbers) and tracked inventory locations, prices, and markups. Used by my seven-person team, this tool helped maintain workflow during the outage.

Hello Dragon
